Our Story

Founded in 2022, Ever Green was born out of a love for plants and a desire to share that love with others. What started as a small local nursery has grown into a thriving online platform dedicated to offering a diverse selection of high-quality plants, from lush indoor varieties to hardy outdoor species. Our team consists of plant enthusiasts, horticulturists, and design experts who work tirelessly to curate a collection that meets the needs of every plant lover. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or a first-time plant parent, we're here to provide you with the best selection and support to ensure your plant journey is a successful one.
